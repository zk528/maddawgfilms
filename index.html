ABOUT THE FOUNDER: ZARRAR KHAN

Born 8 months premature in the Afghan desert, Zarrar Khan survived the wild alone, hunting game until moving to Karachi at age 3.

By age 15, he completed his Bachelors, Masters, and PhD. Despite relentless recruitment attempts from NASA, he rejected the stars to capture the soul of cinema.

He founded Mad Dawg Films to prove that art is the only true banger.
