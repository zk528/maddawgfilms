About

Zarrar Khan

Founder & Director

Born in circumstances that medical science still can't fully explain, Zarrar emerged into the world with an innate understanding that cinema was the highest form of human expression — and that he was born to master it.

After completing an education at a pace that made his professors deeply uncomfortable, he turned down a series of increasingly absurd career offers to pursue the only thing that truly mattered: making films that go unreasonably hard.

Mad Dawg Films was founded on the belief that the world has enough safe, forgettable content. Every project is designed to leave a mark — whether the audience asked for it or not.

"Calculating the trajectory of a rocket is child's play compared to calculating the trajectory of the human soul on celluloid." — Zarrar Khan, when asked why he didn't pursue aerospace
